The Best Movies According to Faizah

MyTop 3 Movies of all time

Sinners

Sinners is a supernatural horror film written, directed, and produced by Ryan Coogler, set in 1932 in the Mississippi Delta. It follows twin brothers, both played by Michael B. Jordan, who return home to start a new life but encounter a sinister supernatural force tied to their past. The film blends horror, music, and Southern Gothic themes to explore identity, community, and redemption.

Wicked

Wicked is a musical fantasy film directed by Jon M. Chu, adapted from the Broadway hit of the same name. It tells the origin story of Elphaba, the future Wicked Witch of the West, and her unlikely friendship with Glinda in the magical Land of Oz. The film explores themes of identity, power, and misunderstood “wickedness” while bringing the story to life through large-scale musical numbers and vivid world-building.

Barbie

Barbie is a fantasy comedy film that follows Barbie as she leaves her perfect, idealized Barbie Land to explore the real world after experiencing unexpected flaws. Guided by curiosity and accompanied by Ken, she discovers what it means to be human, confronting themes of identity, purpose, and societal expectations. The film mixes humor, emotion, and satire to reimagine the iconic doll’s story in a modern, relatable way
